02 — Get to Know Him

Jemi, Kind & Loving

Two versions of the same person — the one the world sees, and the one only I get.

a little bit childish

Jemi was still a bit childish at 17 — but in the cutest way. We started out as deskmates. We'd often call each other at night, play games together. And underneath it all, she was incredibly caring and kind, even before I really knew her.

we grew together

From being chairmates, from calling every night, from playing games until morning — we grew together. Each learned from the other. And I don't know when exactly, something changed. Slowly, without drama.

something that hasn't changed

Even though a lot has changed, one thing remains the same from then until now: he's always there. He always cares. That's something you can't fake, and that's what I admire most.

a man at 22

Jemi, at 22, is much more mature. More mentally aware of himself and those around him. he's smart — not just in academics, but in the way he processes the world.

still him, deeper

Still childish at the right moments. Still clingy in his own way. But when I'm with him, I feel incredibly protected. There's a sense of security I can't quite describe with ordinary words.

smart, dedicated, growing

What I see most in Jemi 22 is his dedication. To what he believes in, to the people he loves, to the dreams he's slowly pursuing. That's not something everyone has.

the clown, the funny one

People see him as the one who always makes people laugh. The one who's never serious. The one who always has a joke at the most unexpected moment. The "clown" of the group and he's really good at it.

but smart and dedicated at the same time

What people often don't realize underneath it all, he's very serious when it comes to things that are important to him. Smart, dedicated, and with a clearer head than most people think.

responsible, selfless

What I see is a very responsible Jemi sometimes even so selfless that it makes me annoyed. He puts others before himself in a very natural way, not because he's forced to.

so caring

He's too kind. He takes care of me in ways I didn't even know I needed, and that's what makes him different.

he is warm. he is everything.

If I had to describe Jemi to someone who's never met him, I'd say: he's warm. Not a performative kind of warm, but a warmth that reaches down to the bones. He's everything a girl could ever ask for. And I'm the lucky one to know that.
